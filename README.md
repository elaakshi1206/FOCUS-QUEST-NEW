# FocusQuest 🎮📚

**FocusQuest** is a **gamified learning mobile application** designed to help students improve their **focus, study habits, and understanding of academic subjects** through an engaging **game-like experience**. Instead of traditional study apps, FocusQuest transforms learning into an **interactive adventure** where students explore worlds, complete quests, unlock characters, and earn rewards while studying.

The platform combines **education, gamification, and focus tracking** to create a personalized learning journey for students from **1st to 10th grade**.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

FocusQuest aims to solve common learning problems such as:

* Lack of focus while studying
* Boredom with traditional study methods
* Low engagement with educational content
* Difficulty understanding topics

To address these challenges, FocusQuest introduces **game mechanics into learning**, making studying feel like playing an adventure game.

Students progress through **levels, maps, and themed worlds** while completing learning tasks such as watching educational videos, reading notes, and solving quizzes.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### 🎮 Gamified Learning Experience

* Learning is structured like a **game adventure**
* Students progress through **levels, maps, and quests**
* Achievements, rewards, and XP encourage consistent learning

### 🌍 Immersive Theme Worlds

The app includes **three interactive worlds based on student grade levels**:

* **Ocean Pirate Adventure (Grades 1–4)**
  Explore islands and oceans with pirate characters and sea creatures.

* **Space Explorer (Grades 5–7)**
  Travel across planets and galaxies while unlocking knowledge.

* **Futuristic Mind Lab (Grades 8–10)**
  Solve advanced challenges in a futuristic digital lab environment.

Each world includes **unique maps, characters, and visual styles**.

---

### 🧠 Adaptive Learning System

FocusQuest dynamically adjusts the learning experience based on student behavior:

* Easier quizzes if the student struggles
* Harder questions when focus and accuracy are high
* Video length adjustments based on engagement
* Intelligent hints and concept reviews when mistakes occur

This ensures **personalized learning for every student**.

---

### 🎥 Smart Video Learning

Each topic begins with a **short educational video lesson** sourced from trusted educational content.

Features include:

* Video-based learning (5–10 minutes)
* Timestamp guidance when mistakes occur
* Automatic replay of relevant concept segments
* Optional shorter videos if the student loses engagement

---

### 📝 Structured Learning Flow

Each topic follows a clear learning structure:

1. **Video Lesson** – Concept explanation
2. **Notes** – Key summaries and important points
3. **Quiz** – Interactive multiple-choice questions

This structure reinforces understanding and improves retention.

---

### 🗺️ Adventure Map Progression

Topics are organized into **game-style maps** where students progress through levels.

Maps include:

* Level checkpoints
* Reward chests
* XP boosters
* Boss challenges
* Achievement milestones

---

### 🎁 Reward & Progression System

Students earn rewards while learning:

* 🪙 Coins
* ⭐ XP points
* 🎁 Reward chests
* 🏆 Achievements

Rewards unlock:

* New characters
* Avatar customization
* Accessories
* Vehicles

---

### 🔥 Focus Tracking System

FocusQuest measures student engagement using a **Focus Score**, calculated from:

* Video completion rate
* Quiz accuracy
* Response time
* Interruptions during learning

Students can track their focus progress through visual analytics and streak systems.

---

### 🎯 Daily Missions

Students receive **daily learning missions** to maintain consistency.

Examples:

* Complete 2 topics
* Focus for 20 minutes
* Achieve 80% quiz accuracy

Completing missions provides **XP, coins, and rewards**.

---

### 👤 Avatar & Character Customization

Students can personalize their learning experience by unlocking and customizing characters.

Customization includes:

* Character skins
* Outfits
* Accessories
* Vehicles

---

### 📊 Learning Analytics Dashboard

The app provides insights into student learning behavior:

* Focus trends
* Quiz accuracy
* Best study times
* Distraction patterns

These insights help students build **better study habits**.
